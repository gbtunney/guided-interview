# Reptile & Aquarium Care Apps — Feature Survey and Market Landscape

> **Purpose**: A non-competitive reference survey of reptile, aquarium, and animal husbandry applications. This document compiles *developer-stated features* alongside *personal notes* (clearly marked), to support feature mapping, terminology normalization, and gap analysis.

---

## Standard Entry Template
Each app below follows a consistent structure to keep terminology and comparisons aligned.

- **Platform / Availability**
- **Target Audience**
- **Primary Focus**
- **Developer-Stated Feature List**
- **Observed / Notable UI or System Features**
- **Personal Notes** *(explicitly marked; may be blank if not used)*

---

## Husbandry Pro

- **Platform / Availability**: iOS, Web
- **Target Audience**: Reptile breeders, advanced keepers, rack-system users
- **Primary Focus**: Comprehensive husbandry records, breeding, inventory, and integrations

### Developer-Stated Feature List
- Animal records and profiles
- Feeding schedules and reminders
- Weight charts and growth tracking
- QR code label creation and scanning
- Racks / cages management
- Feeder inventory tracking
- Incubation tracking
- Breeding plans and breeder loans
- Calendar and scheduling
- Multi-user access
- Optional public URLs for animals for sale
- Data sharing integrations (e.g., MorphMarket, Spyder Robotics)
- Data encryption (package-dependent)

### Observed / Notable Features
- Dense, enterprise-style UI
- Strong emphasis on structured data and automation
- Hardware integration support (e.g., Spyder Robotics / Herpstat)

### Personal Notes
- **Used**: Yes (previously)
- Perceived as powerful but field-heavy
- Noted as an improvement over ReptiScan, though potentially overwhelming

## ReptiScan

- **Platform / Availability**: iOS, Web
- **Target Audience**: Breeders, rack-based collections
- **Primary Focus**: Breeding records, feeding, and transfer documentation

### Developer-Stated Feature List
- Precise feeding and refused-feed tracking
- Clutch tracking (laid date, hatch date)
- Pairing and separation logs
- Weight and length statistics
- QR code scanning for animals and racks
- Single-scan rack feeding
- Web system with backups
- Data export (PDF, Excel)
- Racks / cages organization
- Species categorization
- Custom feeding types

### Observed / Notable Features
- “Sale” feature for transfer of animal records to new owners
- Designed for breeder workflows rather than casual pet keeping

### Personal Notes
- **Used**: Yes (historically)
- QR and transfer concepts considered particularly interesting

## Exotic Reptile Care

- **Platform / Availability**: iOS
- **Target Audience**: First-time owners, experienced keepers, breeders
- **Primary Focus**: Care guidance, reminders, and enclosure/equipment tracking

### Developer-Stated Feature List
- AI-powered reptile species identification
- Unlimited reptile profiles
- Weight, length, and growth tracking
- Shedding and milestone logs
- Photo documentation
- Smart care reminders
- Environmental tracking:
  - Basking spot temperatures
  - Humidity logs
  - Lighting schedules
  - UVB exposure
  - Bulb replacement reminders
- Expert care guides (20+ species)
- Offline data storage
- No account required

### Observed / Notable Features
- Enclosure classification system:
  - Terrestrial
  - Arboreal
  - Aquatic
  - Semi-aquatic
  - Rack system
  - Custom
- Equipment inventory with replacement schedules
- Premium analytics (growth, feeding trends, brumation insights)

### Personal Notes
- **Used**: Exploratory only
- Care guides and environmental logging flagged for later investigation
- “Spot temperature” terminology noted as unclear

## Aquarium Manager: Tank Log

- **Platform / Availability**: iOS
- **Target Audience**: Freshwater and marine aquarists
- **Primary Focus**: Aquarium and water parameter tracking

### Developer-Stated Feature List
- Unlimited aquariums and tanks
- Fish, invertebrate, and plant tracking
- Equipment management (filters, lighting, heaters)
- Water parameter logging:
  - pH
  - Temperature
  - Nitrite / Nitrate
  - GH / KH
- Visual charts and history
- Maintenance logs (water changes, cleaning, trimming)
- Event tracking (additions, disease, breeding)
- Reminders and planning
- Built-in encyclopedia
- Species compatibility guidance

### Observed / Notable Features
- Strong water-quality and parameter analytics
- Aquarium-specific vocabulary and models

### Personal Notes
- **Used**: No
- Logged for future aquarium-side reference
